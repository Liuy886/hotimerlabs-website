# HotimerLabs HeyGen / HyperFrames Logo Exploration

## Style Prompt
Create a flat, premium Web3 agency identity for HotimerLabs. The mark should feel modern, strategic, and technology-aware without looking like a generic crypto token, metallic emblem, or neon startup logo. Use simple geometric construction, restrained multi-tone color, and strong small-size readability.

## Colors
- Background: `#05080A`
- Surface: `#0B1012`
- Text: `#F4F1E7`
- Muted text: `#8B8E88`
- Energy accent: `#DDE76A`
- Trust ivory: `#F0EFE4`
- Structure gray-green: `#8FA49B`

## Typography
- Display: `SF Pro Display`, `Helvetica Neue`, Arial, sans-serif
- UI: `SF Pro Text`, `Helvetica Neue`, Arial, sans-serif

## What NOT to Do
- No X-shaped mark.
- No metallic texture, 3D bevel, chrome, or heavy glow.
- No blue/purple neon cyberpunk palette.
- No over-decorated node/orbit/grid storytelling.
- No single-color mark that feels like a placeholder icon.
